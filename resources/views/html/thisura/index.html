About Website name/brand

Brand name is a Platform for Crowdfunding Medical Bills.
The concept here is to fund people who are financially unstable and unable to pay their medical bills,
by connecting them with the people who are willing to donate money for their medical bills.

This project falls under the 3rd Goal of Sustainable Development Goals.
